# THERMOFORA — Vacuum Forming Machines & CAD Drawings

Manufacturer and engineering resource for vacuum forming and thermoforming machines.  
This repository supports the official website: https://vacuumforming3d.com

## What We Do

THERMOFORA designs and manufactures vacuum forming machines for any production scale —  
from compact desktop units (450×450 mm) to large-format industrial systems (up to 3000×2000 mm).  
Our machines are operating in production facilities across 24 countries.

## Products

- **Ready-to-order machines** — fully engineered, commissioned in your facility
- **CAD drawing packages** — complete documentation to build your own machine  
  (3D assembly in STEP/SolidWorks, part drawings, DXF files, electrical & vacuum schematics, PLC software)
